Who We Are

Guiding Your Journey Since 2019

A Trusted Partner for Students and Parents in Bihar & Jharkhand

Established in 2019, Global Educational Advisor is a government-recognized education consultancy dedicated to mentoring students towards successful careers. Based in Jaynagar, Madhubani (Bihar), we understand the challenges faced by local students — especially first-generation college applicants.

We provide honest, transparent, and eligibility-based counseling. With a vast network of partner colleges and universities across India, we help you make informed choices without any misleading promises. Whether you are aiming for Engineering, Medical sciences, Management, or General degree courses, our personal guidance stands by you.
